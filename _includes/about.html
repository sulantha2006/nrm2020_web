The meeting, established by Prof Albert Gjedde in 1997, represents a community of leaders in the field of in-vivo quantification of brain function using Positron Emission Tomography (PET). Having introduced numerous innovations, the NMR established the reputation to be one of the most impactful and influential conference in the field of brain PET. In 2020, this biannual conference will be held in the vibrant city of Montreal, Canada, and will be hosted by the McGill University, the McGill Centre for Studies in Aging, the Montreal Neurological Institute and the Douglas Research Institute.
