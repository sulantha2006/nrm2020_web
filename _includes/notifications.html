We have been attentive to the latest developments on the COVID-19 worldwide. We believe that the progression of this pandemic might affect our meeting this coming June. We would like to consult the NRM community regarding possible changes in the schedule.
Possible changes are,

  • Postpone the regular meeting to 30 November, 01, 02 December 2020
  • Postpone the regular meeting to 09, 10, 11 December 2020
  • Organize an interactive virtual meeting for 09, 10, 11 December 2020
  • Postpone the regular meeting to 2022
We would send an email with the link to add your vote in the coming days. Independent to the outcome, we will cancel all existing registrations and will refund all registration fees. We will also re-open the abstract webpage for those who would like to update their work.

Finally, we would like to thank everyone who sent abstracts, abstracts reviewers, our sponsors and to everyone who has already registered for the meeting. We will do our best to rectify this situation.

We wish you all well and continued good health during these uncertain times,

NRM 2020 LOC
